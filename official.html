**L-** Found primarily in tea leaves, L-Theanine is an amino acid that promotes relaxation without causing drowsiness. It works synergistically with caffeine to enhance cognitive performance, reduce stress, and improve sleep quality.

**** An essential trace mineral, chromium is crucial for macronutrient metabolism and the regulation of blood sugar levels. It enhances the action of insulin, thereby supporting energy production and maintaining stable blood sugar levels.

Vitamin B12

**Vitamin ** Vital for nerve tissue health, brain function, and the production of red blood cells, Vitamin B12 also helps in DNA synthesis and energy metabolism. A deficiency can lead to anemia and neurological disorders.

Cherry
